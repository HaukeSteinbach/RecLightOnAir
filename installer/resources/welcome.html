RecLight 1.0 Beta

A wireless studio status light that syncs with your DAW and glows when you're recording.

This installer places the RecLight audio plug-ins on your Mac:

  • VST3 → /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3
  • Audio Unit (AU) → /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components
  • Standalone app → /Applications

Beta: this is a pre-release version.
